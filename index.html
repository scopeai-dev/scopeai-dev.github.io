Intelligence systems for media production risk analysis

ScopeAI is building hardware-integrated AI architecture for risk analysis, project scope intelligence, and logistics forecasting across global media production — from feature films to OTT, documentaries, and corporate content.

MSME Registered · Concept Stage · Seeking Pilot Partners

The problem we're solving

Global film, OTT, and television productions face massive financial losses due to decisions made on intuition rather than intelligence. Budgets escalate without early warning. Logistics fail due to poor forecasting. Creative ambition conflicts with physical feasibility. Safety and compliance are addressed reactively, never proactively.

No unified risk system

There is no standardised, intelligent, real-time risk analysis system dedicated exclusively to media production anywhere in the world.

Informal decision-making

Production risks are assessed through experience, intuition, limited spreadsheets, and outdated market reports — not structured intelligence.

Financial exposure

Hundreds of completed films remain unreleased due to creative-commercial misalignment that could have been identified at the script stage.

Compliance gaps

Women safety, child welfare, environmental impact, and labour compliance are fragmented across departments with no unified monitoring.

What we're building

A dedicated hardware system with embedded AI intelligence — acting as a secure, domain-trained decision intelligence terminal for media production risk governance. This is research-grade infrastructure, not a SaaS dashboard.

R

Risk intelligence

Multi-layered risk analysis across creativity, logistics, finance, scheduling, compliance, and market timing — with five-year historical learning and one-year predictive forecasting.

S

Scope validation

Project scope intelligence that cross-verifies creative ambition against budget reality, logistics constraints, and market conditions before capital is committed.

M

Mitigation architecture

Automated mitigation pathway simulation covering creative-logistics conflicts, insurance exposure, environmental impact, and workforce safety indicators.

Research domains: Feature films, OTT series, TV production, documentaries, corporate films, advertising, institutional media, event documentation, and all sectors where audiovisual creation intersects with planning, budgeting, and execution.

Societal integration: Women empowerment and safety scoring, child welfare compliance, labour safety monitoring, environmental footprint forecasting, and constitutional compliance — built in as risk sub-modules, not add-ons.
